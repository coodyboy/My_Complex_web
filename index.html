Earth:

We all know about Earth. That's what we think but did you know that Earth is aproximately 4.5 billon years old. It is worth noting that Earth's atmosphere is composed mainly of nitrogen (about 78%) and oxygen (about 21%) with trace amounts of other gases.

Mars:

Mars is often called the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance, caused by iron oxide (rust) on its surface.A day on Mars is just a little over 24 hours.

Jupiter:

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system, more than 11 times wider than Earth and over 300 times more massive. It's mostly made up of hydrogen and helium.
